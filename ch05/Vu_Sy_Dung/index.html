Our Mission

San Joaquin Valley Town Hall is a non-profit organization that is run by an all-volunteer board of director. Our mission is to bring nationally and internationally renowned, thought-provoking speakers who inform, educate and entertain our audience! As one or our members told us:

Each Year I give a ticket package to each of our family members, i think of it as the gift of knowledge... and that is priceless.
